Job Description

We are seeking a motivated and detail-oriented Business Development Intern to focus on lead generation and contact information research. As an intern, you will play a crucial role in identifying and qualifying potential clients for our niche PLM software services.

Key Responsibilities

  • Outbound Lead Generation:
    • Research and identify potential clients in the manufacturing and engineering industries.
    • Utilize various online tools and databases to find relevant companies and decision-makers.
    • Develop and implement effective lead generation strategies.
  • Inbound Lead Generation:
    • Contribute to content creation for our blog and social media channels.
    • Optimize content for SEO to attract organic traffic.
    • Monitor and respond to inquiries from potential clients.
  • Contact Information Research:
    • Conduct thorough research to find accurate contact information for key decision-makers within target companies.
    • Utilize online tools and databases to validate and update contact information.
  • Data Entry:
    • Accurately input lead information into our CRM system.
    • Maintain and update the CRM with relevant data.

Qualifications

  • Pursuing a degree in business, engineering, or a related field.
  • Strong research skills, including the ability to utilize online tools and databases.
  • Detail-oriented.
  • Proficiency in using computer software and online tools.
  • Self-motivated.
